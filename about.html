Vision & Mission

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To lead the SME sector in Niger State by driving sustainable economic growth through innovative support for businesses in agriculture, tourism, and ICT, fostering entrepreneurship, and improving the quality of life for communities today and in the future.

Our Mission

  1. Innovation:We are committed to driving innovation across industries by empowering small businesses with cutting-edge solutions, training, and development programs.
  2. Sustainability: We promote sustainable practices by supporting eco-friendly business models and helping SMEs reduce their environmental impact through smart business strategies.
  3. Growth & Support: We prioritize the growth and success of our SMEs by providing comprehensive support, from funding opportunities to personalized consultancy, ensuring long-term business stability and prosperity.
